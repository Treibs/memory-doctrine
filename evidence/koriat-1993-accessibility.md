# Koriat 1993 — The Accessibility Model of FOK

Establishes that confidence is a **meta-level inference parasitic on retrieval fluency**, not a direct readout of truth. Counting both correct and incorrect accessible information, the FOK rises whenever much is accessible, regardless of accuracy. Used by [[C1-confidence-earned]] to ground the prohibition on using fluency, recency, or similarity as confidence inputs.
