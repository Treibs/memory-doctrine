# Ramsey 1926 — Truth and Probability

Introduced the betting / Dutch-book interpretation of credences, establishing that rational belief is graded and must satisfy the probability axioms on pain of guaranteed loss. This is the formal licence for SPEC's `confidence: 0.0–1.0` field and is used by [[C1-confidence-earned]].
