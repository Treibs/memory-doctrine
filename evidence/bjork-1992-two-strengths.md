# Bjork & Bjork 1992 — A New Theory of Disuse

Establishes the two-strength split that grounds [[C2-three-orderings]]: evidential confidence (≈ storage strength) and retrievability (≈ retrieval strength) are *separate registers*. Disuse decays retrieval strength only; evidential confidence changes only when new evidence arrives. This dissociation is what makes the three-ordering firewall in C2 non-redundant.
