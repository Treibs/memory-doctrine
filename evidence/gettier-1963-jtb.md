# Gettier 1963 — Is Justified True Belief Knowledge?

Three-page paper that refuted the classical JTB analysis with counterexamples: a belief can be true and fully justified yet fail to constitute knowledge because its truth is coincidental to the justification. Used by [[C1-confidence-earned]] as the formal warning that confidence (= justification) is not truth-tracking; provenance and reliability must be tracked independently.
