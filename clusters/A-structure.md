# A · Structure

Knowledge is positional: a node's meaning is constituted entirely by its pattern of connections, but those connections are not free — every edge spends from a finite retrieval budget (the fan law), so sparse, high-signal links outperform indiscriminate linking. Each node must capture exactly one truth (atomicity), and the network's roots are justified by a foundherentist crossword of mutual support plus external grounding, not by an immune foundational core — so even the most entrenched axioms are revisable given sufficient evidential pressure.
