# E · Method

Building a KPM means distilling the generator — the irreducible source entropy — on top and preserving elaboration beneath it; Shannon's rate-distortion bound is the mathematical floor: you can strip recoverable redundancy losslessly but cannot compress below the joint entropy H without priced distortion. Retrieval practice at delay strengthens the package lifecycle; spaced re-validation must be built into the authoring loop, not treated as a post-hoc step. Every candidate axiom passes a mechanical structural gate (lint: atomicity, evidence presence, frontmatter-body sync) before reaching the judgment gate: adversarial verification, the anti-Gettier guardrail that confirms confidence was earned by an independent, non-circular justification chain. When a query hits an impasse, the resolution path is itself a candidate new generator — the KPM self-compiles, guarded by the same adversarial pass to prevent over-general chunks.
